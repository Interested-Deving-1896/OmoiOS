SessionChannel parity
---------------------
Both implementations expose `.on(type, fn)`, `.send(msg)`, `.close()`, plus
`.open()` for explicit lifecycle (Python only adds `__aenter__`/`__aexit__`
for context-manager sugar; TS doesn't need it since `close()` is sync).

Conclusion
----------
PASS — method-for-method parity, modulo the snake_case ↔ camelCase convention
for `list_all` ↔ `listAll`. No gaps either direction.
